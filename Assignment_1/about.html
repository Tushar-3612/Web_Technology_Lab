Information About Scheme

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) is a government scheme that provides
financial assistance to farmers in case of crop loss. It aims to compensate for
crop losses due to natural calamities, pests and diseases.

Under this scheme, the farmer gets the benefit of crop insurance at a low
premium and in case of loss, the compensation is deposited directly into the bank account.
